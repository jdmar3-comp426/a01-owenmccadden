The Minotaur

Home Videos Photos
  • The Minotaur is a mythical creature from classical Greek mythology
  • The beast is portrayed as having the head of a bull and the body of a man
  • The Labyrinth, designed by Daedalus and Icarus was the home of the minotaur
  • According to legend, the minotaur was killed by Athenian hero Theseus
  • The minotaur is widely referenced in art, literature, and pop culture. Some prominent references include Dante's inferno, Pablo Picasso's Vollard Suite, Rick Riordan's Percy Jackson series
